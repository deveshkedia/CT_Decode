CT-DECODE

A web application that reads Lung CT Scan Images and detects lesions tn the patients’ Lungs

Why US?

It is a well-known fact that during the difficult time of the pandemic, healthcare systems have failed because the number of patients surpassed the available medical facility.
The health care providers must prioritize the critical patients. This web app enables radiologists, doctors, and even a layman to be able to interpret CT scans and this enables the health care workers to know who needs urgent attention thereby saving critical time.
Most COVID patients develop lesions as an after-effect and need urgent attention. If they aren’t treated immediately, it might prove to be fatal. Thus my solution will help in prioritizing the critical patients by telling the patient if lesions are present or not and the affected percentage and also the care or treatment that needs to be undergone.
